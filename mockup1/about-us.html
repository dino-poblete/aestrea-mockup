Aestrea

 

From Greek mythology goddess named Astraea. Daughter of Zeus. She was the bringer of hope and returner of balance. According to legend, Astraea will one day come back to Earth, bringing with her the return of the utopian Golden Age of which she was the ambassador.

 

We believe in the value it represents

We see a lot of wrongs with the world.... and we don't want to stand just idly by.

We want to bring hope to those who have not... and return the balance in society-

With our power and passion to create.

Aestrea was formed by young Filipino professionals who all have the same passion to make a change and help people live an easier life. We would like to establish a company that all can be proud of, and we are confident that, by working together, and having the same principles, we will build the best software development company in the world.

We are passionate to innovate. We excel in providing smart solutions to businesses. Solutions that increase business process efficiency and productivity by delivering high quality software and services through the use of latest technologies and methodologies.

We use the best practices, write clean code, implement test-driven development, agile methodologies, and agile development frameworks in delivering highly effective software.

MISSION

Aestrea exists to synergistically build and provide high quality software consulting and development services to their clientele to help them improve business efficiency and productivity seamlessly by leveraging cutting-edge technologies.

VISION

Aestrea shares a common vision to be internationally known for its great and extraordinary engineers that deliver amazing work.

 

We believe that these principles are the core to our success
